About me

Who am I?

Hey there! It's great to be here, and I'm doing well, thank you for asking. I'm Vishaal Saravanan, a 22-year-old pursuing a Masters in Artificial Intelligence with a minor in Bioinformatics. Ever since I used a computer for the first time at the age of eight, I've been fascinated by how technology works, especially the AI Bot J.A.R.V.I.S. from my favorite superhero, Iron Man.

Studying computing and math in secondary school fueled my passion, and I eventually enrolled in a bachelor's in the computer science program. During my second year, my peers and I developed an intra-college social media platform that connected students and teachers, which was well received on campus. Recognizing the demand for small-scale applications in the business community, we established "Tuloops,"(Well, inspired by two loops forming infinity) a student startup- team dedicated to rapidly developing SAAS and AI-enabled solutions. Since then, we have used Tuloops to connect with people and save its earnings in our little knowledge bank.

As an AI enthusiast, I'm not only passionate about technology, but I'm also deeply concerned about the environment. I firmly believe that technology, particularly AI, can play a critical role in promoting sustainability and fighting climate change. Recent research has shown that AI can optimize energy consumption, improve agricultural practices, and monitor environmental pollution, among other things. My dream is to see a future where AI is seamlessly integrated with sustainable practices, making AI-enabled technologies accessible to everyone, and helping us build a more resilient and greener planet.
